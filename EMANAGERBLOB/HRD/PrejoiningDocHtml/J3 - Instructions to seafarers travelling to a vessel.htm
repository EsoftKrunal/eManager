Instructions to Seafarers Travelling to a Vessel

(Nationality: Ukrainian / Russians / Latvian / Filipino)

In all cases:

  • Contact your Crewing Officer at $ManningAgent$ in case you are in doubt about your travel arrangement or precautions to be taken.
  • Before leaving home contact your $ManningAgent$ Crewing Officer to confirm you start travelling and receive last minute updates (if any)
    1. Transport to Airport

      During transport to the departure airport we require you to:

      1. Comply with the standard infection protection and control precautions as listed in Attachment 1
      2. Keep social distance of at least 1 meter
      3. Avoid close contacts with person who are or appear unwell or show any COVID-19 symptoms
      4. Wear mask and gloves
      5. You must at all times carry your own luggage
      6. Keep all documents listed in Attachment 2 in separate bag or compartment that can be easily accessed and disinfected later
      7. Show all documents for inspection and verification when requested by authorities
      8. Follow the travel instructions as advised by your Crewing Officer and only use the taxis, buses etc. as advised by the Crewing Officer
      9. Depending on the duration of the transfer continue to twice daily check and record your temperature using attachment 3 (Form J8 - Daily Crew Temperature Check Records During Travel)
    2. Departure Airport

      During your stay at the departure airport we require you to:

      1. Comply with the standard infection protection and control precautions as listed in Attachment 1
      2. Keep social distance of at least 1 meter
      3. Avoid close contacts with person who are or appear unwell or show any COVID-19 symptoms
      4. Wear mask and gloves
      5. You must at all times carry your own luggage
      6. Keep all documents listed in Attachment 2 in separate bag or compartment that can be easily accessed and disinfected later
      7. Show all documents for inspection and verification when requested by authorities
      8. Depending on the duration of the stay at the airport to twice daily check and record your temperature using attachment 3.
      9. Follow the any specific instructions as advised by your Crewing Officer
    3. During Flight

      During your flight we require you to:

      1. Comply with the instructions and procedures of the airline and cabin crew of the aircraft
      2. Keep social distancing to the extent possible from other passengers on the flight and sit with appropriate seat spacing as maybe arranged by the airline or cabin crew onboard the aircraft.
      3. Comply with the standard infection protection and control precautions as listed in Attachment 1
      4. Avoid close contacts with person who are or appear unwell or show any COVID-19 symptoms
      5. Wear mask and gloves
      6. Limit exposure to the extent possible to aircraft crew during in-flight service and other passengers when using toilets.
      7. Handle your own luggage on board the aircraft
      8. Depending on the duration of the flight continue to twice daily check and record your temperature using attachment 3.
    4. Arrival Airport

      During your stay at the arrival airport we require you to:

      1. Comply with the instructions and procedures of the airport and relevant local authorities, including any health screening requirements such as temperature checks
      2. Comply with the standard infection protection and control precautions as listed in Attachment 1
      3. Keep social distance of at least 1 meter
      4. Avoid close contacts with person who are or appear unwell or show any COVID-19 symptoms
      5. Wear mask and gloves
      6. Depending on the duration of the stay at the airport continue to twice daily check and record your temperature using attachment 3.
    5. Hotel Stay and transfer to/from hotel (if included in your travel arrangements)

      If your travel at any point include a stay in a hotel then we require you to:

      1. Transfer to/from hotel
        1. Comply with instructions and procedures from relevant local or national authorities which might potential include the need to comply with instructions regarding self-isolation or quarantine in a hotel, or another place of accommodation that might be determined by local and national authorities
        2. Depending on the duration of the transfer continue to twice daily check and record your temperature using attachment 3.
        3. Comply with the standard infection protection and control precautions as listed in Attachment 1
        4. Keep social distance of at least 1 meter
        5. Avoid close contacts with person who are or appear unwell or show any COVID-19 symptoms
        6. Wear mask and gloves
        7. You must at all times carry your own luggage
        8. Keep all documents listed in Attachment 2 in separate bag or compartment that can be easily accessed and disinfected later
        9. Show all documents for inspection and verification when requested by authorities
        10. Follow the travel instructions as advised by your Crewing Officer and only use the taxis, buses etc. as advised by the Crewing Officer
      2. During Hotel Stay
        1. Comply with all procedures of the hotel as well as national or local authorities paying special attention to social distancing, hygiene and PPE requirements
        2. Only leave you room for meals, visits to $ManningAgent$ office, medical center or other strictly required reason
        3. Comply with the standard infection protection and control precautions as listed in Attachment 1
        4. Avoid close contacts with person who are or appear unwell or show any COVID-19 symptoms
        5. Depending on the duration of the transfer continue to twice daily check and record your temperature using attachment 3.
        6. Inform $ManningAgent$ if you show any Covid-19 symptoms.
        7. Follow the any specific instructions as advised by your Crewing Officer
    6. Transit to Joining Port

      During transit to the port we require you to:

      1. Comply with instructions and procedures from relevant local or national authorities which might potential include the need to comply with instructions regarding self-isolation or quarantine in a hotel, or another place of accommodation that might be determined by local and national authorities
      2. Comply with the standard infection protection and control precautions as listed in Attachment 1
      3. Keep social distance of at least 1 meter
      4. Avoid close contacts with person who are or appear unwell or show any COVID-19 symptoms
      5. Wear mask and gloves
      6. You must at all times carry your own luggage
      7. Keep all documents listed in Attachment 2 in separate bag or compartment that can be easily accessed and disinfected later
      8. Show all documents for inspection and verification when requested by authorities
      9. Follow the travel instructions as advised by your Crewing Officer and only use the taxis, buses etc. as advised by the Crewing Officer
      10. Depending on the duration of the transfer continue to twice daily check and record your temperature using attachment 3.
    7. At the Joining Port

      During your stay and movement ashore at the joining port we instruct you to:

      1. Comply with the standard infection protection and control precautions as listed in Attachment 1
      2. Keep social distance of at least 1 meter
      3. Avoid close contacts with person who are or appear unwell or show any COVID-19 symptoms
      4. Wear mask and gloves
      5. You must at all times carry your own luggage
      6. Keep all documents listed in Attachment 2 in separate bag or compartment that can be easily accessed and disinfected later
      7. Show all documents for inspection and verification when requested by authorities
      8. Depending on the duration of the transfer continue to twice daily check and record your temperature using attachment 3.
    8. Embarkation

      When you embark the ship then we require you to:

      1. Avoid close contact and non-essential interaction with other personnel on the quayside, on the gangway or in the launch boat, including other seafarer
      2. Keep social distance of at least 1 meter
      3. Avoid close contacts with person who are or appear unwell or show any COVID-19 symptoms
      4. Wear mask and gloves
      5. You must at all times carry your own luggage
      6. Keep all documents listed in Attachment 2 in separate bag or compartment that can be easily accessed and disinfected later
      7. Show all documents for inspection and verification when requested by authorities
      8. Depending on the duration of the transfer continue to twice daily check and record your temperature using attachment 3.
      9. Follow owners/operators Covid-19 onboard plans and instructions given by the vessels Master
    9. After Embarkation / The First Period Onboard
      1. Follow owners/operators Covid-19 onboard plans and instructions given by the vessels Master
