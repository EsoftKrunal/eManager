Instructions to Joining Seafarers

(Nationality: Ukrainian / Russians / Latvian / Filipino)

You are now lined-up to join a vessel. Due to Covid19 pandemic this joining will be different from your previous joining and travel as there are additional instructions and information which are to fully understood by you and which you have to strictly comply with.

The reason for these additional instructions and information to you is to mitigate (limit) risk of brining the virus onboard and fulfill requirements by airports, airlines, flag authorities and the owners of the vessels.

You have now to carefully read and comply the following. If you have any questions then please ask $ManningAgent$.

Attachment No. Topic
1 General information on coronavirus (COVID-19) and standard infection protection based on WHO guidance
2 List of symptoms of coronavirus (COVID-19)
3 Detailed information on the ship owners’ coronavirus (COVID-19) plans and procedures
4 Rules on social distancing, self-isolation which you need to keep at least 14 days from before starting travelling to the vessel
5 Before joining you will have to sign a “Crew Health Self-Declaration”. We attach here an example. You should not sign now but familiarize yourself with the requirements.
6 Daily Crew Temperature Check Records
7 List of personal protective equipment to be brought by you.

You are not allowed to have any close contract with any persons who are or appear unwell or show COVID-19 symptoms (e.g. cough, fever, etc.)

You are from now on requested to check your temperature twice daily and keep records of the temperature using the attachment 6 hereto: “Daily Crew Temperature Check Records” and immediately inform $ManningAgent$ if you have any increased temperature.

You must immediately inform $ManningAgent$ if you have any symptoms or if any persons who are in contact with you show any symptoms.

Before starting travel you must buy the personal protective equipment (PPE) as listed in attachment 7. You must bring the listed PPE items with you and use them when travelling to the vessel. If you need money to buy the items then please contact $ManningAgent$. If you are not able to buy the items then also please contact $ManningAgent$. Please keep all receipts for your purchases and give them to the Master when you have joined. The costs will be refunded together with your first allotment.

3 days before joining you will have to pass a Corvid-19 test. You will later receive more information where and when this will take place.

By my signature below I confirm that I have understood all of the above and that I will strictly comply with same. In case of any questions or if I for whatever reason is not able or did not comply then I will immediately contact $ManningAgent$.
